      Privacy Policy

      Last updated: January 2025

      No Data Collection

      AI Food Nutrition Scanner does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All food analysis happens locally in your browser or via anonymous API calls. We do not store your name, email, location, or any identifying information.

      Image Usage

      When you scan food using the camera or upload an image, it is processed locally or sent to OpenRouter AI API for analysis only. Images are NOT stored on any server. They are processed momentarily and discarded after generating nutritional estimates.

      Local Storage

      This app uses your device's localStorage to save scan history (last 10 entries), daily protein tracking data, user preferences (dark mode, language), and your API key (stored locally only). This data never leaves your device.

      Advertising

      This app may display advertisements from Adstera and other third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies and device identifiers to serve relevant ads. Please review Adstera's privacy policy for more information.

      Educational Disclaimer

      All nutritional information is for educational purposes only. AI-generated values are estimates and may not be accurate. Do not use this app for medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified nutritionist or doctor.

      Children's Privacy

      This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

      About Us

      AI Food Nutrition Scanner

      Our Mission

      NutriScan AI was created to make nutritional awareness accessible to everyone. By leveraging the power of AI vision models via OpenRouter, we provide instant, educational nutritional insights for thousands of food items — completely free of charge.

      AI Technology

      This app uses OpenRouter AI API with advanced vision models (Gemini 2.0 Flash, Llama Vision, GPT-4o, Claude) to analyze food images and generate detailed nutritional information in real-time when an API key is configured. Without an API key, it uses a local estimated database.

      Key Features

      • 📷 AI-powered food scanning via camera
      • 🖼️ Gallery image analysis
      • 📊 Detailed macro & micronutrient data
      • ⚖️ BMI Calculator with health insights
      • 💪 Daily protein intake tracker
      • 📋 Scan history (last 10 items)
      • 🌙 Dark mode support
      • 🇮🇳 Hindi/English language toggle
      • 📤 Share results via native share API
      • 📴 Offline fallback with local database

      Our Values

      Health is wealth. We believe everyone deserves access to nutritional education. Our app is built with privacy-first principles — no data collection, no tracking, just pure nutritional insights.
